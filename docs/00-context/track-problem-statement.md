# Track Problem Statement — Public Sector (Government)

## Official title

**Secure and Efficient Processing of Administrative Documents Across Public Sector Organizations**

Transforming manual workflows into intelligent, secure document management systems.

## Executive Problem Statement (PDF page 1)

### Processing Delays
Manual, multi-step workflows to classify, route, review, and respond result in significant delays.

### Fragmented Flow
Limited visibility into document status and inefficient coordination across departments.

### Security Constraints
Strict security and multi-level classification requirements (Unclassified to Top Secret).

## Key Impact Areas

### Citizen Experience
Slower response times to citizens and stakeholders, reducing overall service quality.

### Compliance Risk
Higher risk of missed deadlines, regulatory non-compliance, or unresolved cases.

### Cost Efficiency
Increased administrative workload and high operational costs due to manual effort.

## Quick numbers (from PDF)

- **High %** — manual processing ratio
- **5–7 days** — average processing time
- **Many+** — departments involved per document
- **4+** — security levels

## Current state — 6-Step Manual Workflow (PDF page 2)

1. **Intake** — documents received via physical channels and digital copies
2. **Registration** — logged at administrative/records management unit
3. **Distribution** — forwarded to relevant departments for processing
4. **Review** — content review, scope determination, summarization
5. **Consultation** — cross-departmental feedback and comments
6. **Response** — final responses issued to citizens/stakeholders

## Digital Transformation Gaps (PDF page 2)

- Many submissions still delivered in hard copy format
- Document identification numbers not fully standardized
- Scanned copies may vary in quality
- Hybrid paper-digital workflows must be supported

## 3 Key Operational Challenges (PDF page 3)

### Challenge 1 — Manual Identification
- **Time-intensive review:** significant time required to read and classify incoming documents
- **Risk of misrouting:** incorrect routing or delayed assignment of documents
- **Increased backlog:** higher volume during intake and distribution phases
- **Key Impacts:** Processing delays, Workflow inefficiency

### Challenge 2 — Cross-Department Consolidation
- **Duplication of effort:** each department reviews the same document independently
- **Inconsistent interpretation:** different departments may interpret information differently
- **Delayed responses:** slow consolidation of information across departments
- **Key Impacts:** Duplication, Inconsistency

### Challenge 3 — Extended Approval Cycles
- **Multiple review layers:** documents proceed through multiple levels of review
- **Repeated consultations:** involving multiple personnel for feedback
- **Limited visibility:** real-time status tracking is difficult
- **Key Impacts:** Prolonged timelines, Slower responses

## 4 Key Operational Constraints (PDF page 4)

### Physical Document Reliance
Many submissions still delivered in hard copy format. Document identification numbers may not be fully standardized. Scanned copies may vary in quality.

### Strict Security & Confidentiality
Administrative data is highly sensitive. Requires restricted access based on user roles, protection against unauthorized exposure, and full audit logging.

### Multi-Level Document Classification
Documents categorized under multiple confidentiality levels with defined access permissions and controlled handling processes.
- Unclassified
- Confidential
- Secret
- Top Secret

### Fragmented Systems
Documents stored across multiple disconnected systems, resulting in difficulty locating historical records and limited interoperability.

## 8 Future-State Capabilities (PDF page 5)

1. **Automated Ingestion** — physical & digital document processing
2. **Intelligent Classification** — document type & subject detection
3. **Auto-Routing** — department identification
4. **AI Summarization** — key information extraction
5. **Cross-Dept Collaboration** — structured workflow
6. **Real-time Tracking** — lifecycle status visibility
7. **Centralized Indexing** — document retrieval
8. **Access Control** — multi-level security
