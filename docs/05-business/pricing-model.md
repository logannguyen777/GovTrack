# Pricing Model

## Principles

1. **Value-based** — pricing reflects customer savings, not our cost
2. **Land and expand** — low barrier to entry, grow with usage
3. **Predictable for gov** — gov budgets need annual line items, so avoid pure consumption pricing
4. **Aligned incentives** — more cases processed = more value = more revenue
5. **Transparent** — no surprise fees

- Time saved per case: ~45 ngày × 10k cases = 450k ngày
- Value per ngày: ~200k VND (SME average)

**ROI for customer: 90 tỉ / 500M = 180× return**

No reasonable customer says no to that ROI.

### For 1 Bộ

## Why not free / freemium?

Tempting but wrong for gov:
- Gov needs accountability → paid contract creates SLA framework
- Free tier would be abused by sub-scale users
- "Free" looks suspicious to procurement (no clear provider)

Better: **Shinhan-funded PoC** as the free-equivalent, formal contract after.

## Payment terms

- **Gov customers:** annual invoice, NET 30
- **Enterprise:** annual invoice or quarterly, NET 30
- **Early adopters (Year 1):** 10% discount for 3-year commitment

## Discounts

- **Multi-year commit:** 10% (3-year), 15% (5-year)
- **Bundle multiple Sở same tỉnh:** 15%
- **Early adopter program (first 5 customers):** 20%
- **Non-profit / social enterprise gov:** case-by-case

## Price defensibility

**"Why 500M VND for 1 Sở?"**

Answers:
1. **Customer savings are 180× the subscription.** Procurement team will check ROI.
2. **Alternative cost:** internal build = 3 tỉ VND + 18 months + uncertain outcome
3. **Integration:** comparable gov platforms cost 1–3 tỉ VND + multiple years to deploy
4. **Compliance ready:** avoid legal risk (un-priced value)
5. **Ongoing updates:** we maintain KG as laws change (price includes this)

## Pricing governance

- **Quarterly pricing review** based on real deployments
- **Price ladder:** never discount published prices without exec approval
- **Customer-specific pricing:** rare, only for strategic logo customers
- **Inflation adjustment:** 5% annual increase after Year 1

## Red lines

**Never:**
- Discount below 40% off list (protects margins)
- Offer free to-be-paid tiers
- Price based on savings percentage (too complex, creates incentive misalignment)
- Complicated consumption-only pricing (gov doesn't do this)

**Always:**
- Annual commitment
- Written contract with DPA
- Clear SLA on our side
- Customer success metrics agreed upfront
