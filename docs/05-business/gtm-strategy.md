# Go-to-Market Strategy

## Phased rollout

### Phase 1 — Prove (Months 1–6)

**Objective:** Deploy production at 1 Sở, demonstrate measurable outcomes.

**Activities:**
- Shinhan InnoBoost PoC framework → legal entity setup
- Partnership MoU với 1 Sở (likely Sở XD or Sở TN&MT of a progressive province)
- Deploy GovFlow for 1 flagship TTHC (e.g., CPXD)
- Measure: SLA compliance, processing time, cán bộ satisfaction, citizen NPS
- **Success criteria:** 50%+ reduction in processing time, 90%+ SLA hit rate, positive testimonials

**Deliverables:**
- Production system with 1 TTHC fully supported
- Published case study with metrics
- Reference customer willing to speak publicly
- Whitepaper on deployment methodology

**Why 1 Sở first:** avoid over-commitment, learn deployment process, build reference.

### Phase 2 — Replicate (Months 7–12)

**Objective:** Expand within the same tỉnh, add adjacent Sở.

**Activities:**
- Same tỉnh: add 2–4 more Sở (XD → TN&MT → KH&ĐT → Tư pháp)
- Land 1 additional flagship tỉnh (neighboring province, leveraging word-of-mouth)
- Add 2–3 more TTHCs to support per Sở
- Formal partnership with 1 Bộ (as advisor/beta)
- Hire: 1 CSM, 1 Solution Architect

**Revenue:** 3–5 Sở × 500M VND = 1.5–2.5 tỉ VND ARR

### Phase 3 — Scale (Year 2)

**Objective:** 5 tỉnh, 3 Bộ, 2 enterprise pilots.

**Activities:**
- Target top 10 tỉnh có nhu cầu CĐS cao (HCMC, Hà Nội, Bình Dương, Đồng Nai, Đà Nẵng, Hải Phòng, Quảng Ninh, Vĩnh Phúc, Bắc Ninh, Long An)
- Partner với 1 system integrator (CMC, HPT, CEH) làm deployment partner
- Launch Bộ-level deployments (Bộ XD, Bộ TN&MT, Bộ KH&ĐT)
- Open enterprise line for VN30 companies (TTHC nội bộ + integration với government filings)
- Hire: 3 sales, 2 CSM, 5 engineers, 1 marketing

**Revenue target:** 10–15 tỉ VND ARR

### Phase 4 — Dominate (Year 3+)

**Objective:** National rollout với strong marketplace position.

**Activities:**
- 20+ tỉnh, all 4 flagship Sở each
- 10+ Bộ deployments
- 20+ enterprise clients
- International expansion exploratory (Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar — similar public admin structures)
- Potentially a fundraise (Series A $5–10M)

## Customer acquisition playbook

### Stage 1 — Awareness (before first contact)
- Published case studies
- Conference presence
- Warm referrals from Shinhan/Alibaba
- LinkedIn content about gov-tech + GraphRAG

### Stage 2 — Interest (first contact → demo)
- Initial 30-min discovery call
- Custom demo video based on their TTHC priorities
- Light deep-dive on technical architecture

### Stage 3 — Evaluation (demo → PoC)
- 2-week technical evaluation
- Co-create PoC scope (1 TTHC, 1 department)
- Success criteria definition
- Legal review (contract + DPA)

### Stage 4 — PoC (2–3 months)
- Deploy limited production
- Weekly check-ins
- Measure against success criteria
- Hand-holding as needed

### Stage 5 — Scale (after PoC success)
- Expand within customer (more Sở, more TTHC)
- Reference customer (testimonials, logo usage)
- Case study publication
- Renewal + upsell

## Risks + mitigations

### Slow procurement
- **Mitigation:** Use Shinhan InnoBoost PoC framework to bypass long RFP
- **Backup:** partner with system integrator who has existing contracts

### Political change
- **Mitigation:** align with Đề án 06 (high-level political backing), NĐ 61 (stable legal framework)

### Tech risk (on-prem LLM performance)
- **Mitigation:** Qwen3-32B proven on modest hardware; cloud fallback via Alibaba Cloud

### Competitive response
- **Mitigation:** first-mover + network effects (more Sở = more KG data = better system)

### Over-customization
- **Mitigation:** platform architecture with config-driven customization, not code-per-customer
